College Clubs

Ri3D At Penn State

(Fall 2023 - Current)

Every year teams of college students race to design and build a robot in three days, that adheres to the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) rules. "FIRST is a global robotics community preparing young people for the future and the world's leading youth-serving nonprofit advancing STEM education." - FIRST 2024

Penn State Robotics

(Fall 2023 - Current)

From just a pile of electronics, students are tasked with designing and creating robots to compete in a competition in just 4 months (one semester). Teams comprised of 2-6 students are provided with a kit of parts, however it's up to the students to buy and or make any other parts necessary to build a winning robot.

Penn State Bowling

(Fall 2023 - Current)

I am a member of the Pennsylvania State University Bowling Team, which travels to other BIG 10 schools to compete in tournaments.

HackPSU

(Fall 2024 - Current)

Hack PSU holds classes and a hackathon every year to help students build real world coding experience.
