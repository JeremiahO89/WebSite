FPV Drone

First Person View Drones (FPV Drones) are used in the industry for cinema capture, drone racing, package delivery, and recreational use. I personally used them as a way to learn about ESCs, Motors, Gyros, Video capture/transmission, and hardware-software integration.

Robotic Arm

Using 55 gram servos and a 28BYJ-48 stepper motor, I built a 6 axis robotic arm with an electromagnet gripper on the end. Designing these parts from scratch and 3d printing them, gave me insight into motor torque and slash which proved to be a constant issue.

❮ ❯

Electric Longboard

Through multiple revisions I made an electric longboard capable of 45mph and 11 miles of range; using a 300A esc, homemade 12s LIPO with BMS, and two 6000w motors. This taught me how to wire high amperage electronics near low voltage electronics and how VESC works.

Shift Register Display

Using SN74HC595N shift registers and a homemade LED sign, I built a sign which only uses 5 digital pins on an Arduino and communicates with the sign using binary signals. I plan on making a newer version which acts like an LED array display.

Robotics Clubs

The Maze Solver robot, works by using a depth first search algorithm to search the entire maze with ultrasonic and infrared sensors. In just 3 months I modeled the entire robot from scratch and 3D printed it in PLA.
