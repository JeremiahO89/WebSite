FPV Drone

First Person View Drones (FPV Drones) are used in the industry for cinema capture, drone racing, package delivery, and recreational use. I personally used them as a way to learn about ESCs, Motors, Gyros, Video capture/transmission, and hardware-software integration.

Robotic Arm

Using 5, 55 gram servos, and 1 28BYJ-48 servo motor, I built a 6 axis robotic arm with an electromagnet gripper on the end. Designing these parts from scrach and 3d printing them gave me an insight into motor torque and slash which proved to be a constant issue.

Electric Longboard

Through multiple revisions I made an electric longboard capable of 45mph and 11 miles of range using a 300A esc, homemade 12s LIPO battery with BMS, and two 6000w motors. This taught me how to wire high amperage electronics near low voltage electronics and use VESC.

Shift Register Display

Using SN74HC595N shift registers and a homemade led array I built a 20x20 display which only uses 5 digital pins on an arduino and communicates with the display using binary signals.
