Electric Longboard

Using a 300A esc, a homemade 12s lipo battery with BMS system, and two 6000 watt motors I built an electric longbaord which is capable of 45 mph and had 11 miles of range. When making this board I learned a lot about proper wiring practices for high amperage applications and how to navigate the VESC software.
