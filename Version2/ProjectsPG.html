FPV Drone

First Person View Drones (FPV Drones) are used in the industry for cinema capture, drone racing, package delivery, and recreational use. I personally used them as a way to learn about ESCs, Motors, Gyros, Video capture/transmission, and hardware-software integration.

Robotic Arm

Using 5, 55 gram servos, and 1 28BYJ-48 servo motor, I built a 6 axis robotic arm with an elextromagnet cripper on the end. Designing these parts from scrach and 3d printing them gave me an insight into motor torque and slash which proved to be a constant issue.
